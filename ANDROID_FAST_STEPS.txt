SAIVEX ANDROID FAST STEPS

After your website is live:

1. Open Android Studio.
2. Open your android/SaivexAndroid project or SaivexAndroid folder.
3. Open app/build.gradle.
4. Change SAIVEX_URL to:

buildConfigField "String", "SAIVEX_URL", "\"https://YOUR_USERNAME.pythonanywhere.com\""

5. Build APK:
   Build -> Build Bundle(s) / APK(s) -> Build APK(s)

6. Build AAB for Play Store:
   Build -> Generate Signed Bundle / APK -> Android App Bundle

Google Play Store requires a paid developer account. Testing APK on your phone is free.
