SAIVEX Android App — Free PWA Method

This is the free method.

After SAIVEX is online with HTTPS:
1. Open SAIVEX URL in Chrome on Android.
2. Tap three dots menu.
3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app".
4. SAIVEX opens like an Android app.

Native Android app later:
Use Android Studio + Kotlin/Flutter and connect to your Flask backend API.
